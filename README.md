# Brijwasi Sweets India Pvt. Ltd. — The Bankelal Group

**Where Timeless Traditions Meet Luxurious Flavours.**

## About Us

Brijwasi Sweets began in 1946 as a small *bhojnalaya* (eatery) in Kalbadevi, Mumbai, 
where a piece of mithai served at the end of a meal won such loyalty from diners that 
the eatery itself transformed into a dedicated sweets store. Founded by **Shri Ramswaroop** 
and his son **Shri Bankelal Goyal**, who had migrated from Brij, Mathura, the brand grew 
from that single outlet into one of Mumbai's most trusted names in Indian sweets and 
savouries — now spanning 14 stores across Mumbai and Navi Mumbai, alongside 7 digital 
storefronts.

In 2006, the family formally established **The Bankelal Group** as an independent 
corporate identity, creating **Brijwasi Sweets India Pvt. Ltd.** to manage retail 
operations and hold the brand's registered trademark. Leadership passed from the 
founding generation to grandson **Shri Mahendra Goyal**, and later to great-grandsons 
**Pratik and Anuj Goyal**, who modernised the business — expanding into exhibitions, 
corporate gifting, and e-commerce.

## Legacy Timeline

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 1946 | Founding recipes developed; the brand's original flavours are preserved to this day |
| 1950 | First to introduce Bengali sweets (Rasgulla, Gulab Jamun) to Mumbai; pioneered home/office delivery |
| 1970 | First in the industry to launch a dedicated customer helpline and loyalty program |
| 1998 | Among the earliest sweet brands to offer e-commerce and online ordering |
| 2006 | The Bankelal Group formally established; Brijwasi Sweets India Pvt. Ltd. incorporated |
| 2015 | Serving government offices, airlines, hotels, corporates, and households nationwide |
| 2021 | Celebrated 75 years of heritage |
| 2023 | Expanded into Navi Mumbai with new stores at Vashi and Seawoods |
| 2024 | Opened its 11th store, at Prabhadevi |
| 2026 | 80 years of tradition — 14 physical stores and 7 digital storefronts strong |
